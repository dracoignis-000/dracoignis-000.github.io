The store offers the following features:

Product Listings: Farmers can create listings for their produce, including detailed descriptions, images, and pricing information.

Search and Filtering: Customers can easily search for specific products or filter listings based on criteria such as price range, location, and organic certification.

Shopping Cart: Customers can add products to their shopping cart for easy checkout. They can review their cart, update quantities, and remove items before making a purchase.

Secure Payments: The store integrates with a secure payment gateway to ensure safe and convenient transactions. Customers can use various payment methods, such as credit cards, debit cards, or digital wallets.

Order Tracking: Once an order is placed, customers can track the status of their shipment. They receive updates on the shipping process, including estimated delivery dates and any delays.

User Reviews: Customers can leave reviews and ratings for products they have purchased, helping other customers make informed decisions. Farmers can also respond to reviews and engage with their customers.

Customer Support: The store provides a customer support system, allowing customers to contact the farmers directly with any questions or concerns regarding their purchases.

Multi-language Support: The store's interface can be translated into different languages to cater to customers from various countries. The language strings are dynamically loaded based on the user's language preferences.

The users can navigate through different sections of the store, including product listings, shopping cart, and customer support, using the intuitive navigation menu.
